Introduction & Dataset

U.S. Prison Population by Race vs General Population Demographics

We analyze racial disparities in U.S. incarceration using two datasets:

  • Prison Population by Race (by state)
  • General Population Demographics (by state)

Goal: Reveal and address bias in incarceration rates.

Why This Matters & Methodology

Biases in these datasets stem from systemic inequalities in the justice system.

  • Disproportionate policing & sentencing
  • Historical discrimination
  • Data collection/reporting issues

How We Analyzed:

  • Data analysis: Pandas, Numpy
  • Visualization: Matplotlib, Seaborn
  • Bias detection: AIF360 toolkit

Recommendations & Conclusion

Recommendations:

  • Expand data coverage and include socioeconomic variables
